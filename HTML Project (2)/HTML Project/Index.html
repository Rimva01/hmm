Rust is a multiplayer survival game developed by Facepunch Studios. It involves player versus player(PVP) combat in which players compete with eachother for materials, food and weaponry in order to survive and thrive. Players also have to contend with hostile wildlife ranging from packs of wolves to bears as well as enemy NPCs (non-player characters) known as Scientists who protect very lucrative weapon caches that the player can search for weapons. The game fully released in Febuary of 2018, but an early access build had been out since December of 2013. You can purchase Rust on steam here
